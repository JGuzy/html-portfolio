My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Rush Hour

All 3 movies are hilarious. If you're into silly humor like me, these movies are for you. The 2nd movie is probably my favorite of the 3

The Fast and the Furious

The whole franchise is good. They went from cool movies about street racing to ridiculous, over-the-top movies about international espionage. What's not to love?
